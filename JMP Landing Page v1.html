01 · COMPANY

Independent.
Operator-owned.
Quietly relentless.

JMP is a third-generation oilfield services company headquartered in Signal Hill, California, with field operations in Santiago, Neuquén, and Bogotá. We move slowly when it matters and quickly when it counts.

Our customers are operators who want a single, accountable partner — not a vendor who disappears between jobs. That is the only way we have ever worked.

02 · SERVICES

Six disciplines.
One field crew.

01
Well intervention

Wireline, slickline, and coiled-tubing operations with crews who have logged decades on Permian and Vaca Muerta formations alike.

 02
Production testing

Surface well testing, separator packages, and flowback with calibrated data hand-off that integrates with your existing reporting stack.

 03
Cementing & stimulation

Primary and remedial cementing, acid stimulation, and bridge plug operations — designed in-house, executed by our own crew.

 04
Plug & abandonment

Onshore P&A programs with regulator-grade documentation. Decommission once, properly, with no return visits.

 05

Day-rate company-man services with bilingual supervisors and a culture of writing things down before the morning meeting.

 06
Logistics & mobilisation

Cross-border equipment moves, customs liaison, and base-camp setup for new pad development in remote acreage.
